In a whirlwind of opulence and fanfare, the pre-wedding festivities of Anant Ambani and Radhika Merchant painted Jamnagar, Gujarat, with vibrant hues of celebration. The grand affair, attended by luminaries from all walks of life, set the stage for the much-anticipated wedding scheduled for July.

Now, whispers of the upcoming nuptials have reached far and wide, with reports indicating that Anant and Radhika will exchange vows at their majestic Stoke Park estate in London. Nita Ambani, the matriarch of the Ambani clan, is reportedly at the helm of preparations, her touch shaping every exquisite detail.

Anant's deep affection for his mother shines through as he credits Nita Ambani as the driving force behind his accomplishments. Her unwavering dedication was evident during the spectacular three-day pre-wedding extravaganza, which she orchestrated single-handedly.

As Bollywood's elite prepare to descend upon London for the main event, India Today reveals that invitations have been extended to the industry's most celebrated stars. While the specifics of the wedding festivities remain under wraps, guests can expect an elaborate display of grandeur and artistry, echoing the diverse themes that graced the pre-wedding celebrations.
