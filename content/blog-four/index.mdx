In a groundbreaking partnership, Cognizant and Microsoft are empowering businesses with the transformative power of Microsoft's Generative AI and Copilots, aiming to revolutionize workflows and elevate customer experiences.

## Cognizant with MS Co pilot

Cognizant has acquired an impressive 25,000 Microsoft 365 Copilot seats, along with specialized Sales and Services Copilots, to enhance efficiency and streamline operations. Their ambitious goal is to deploy Microsoft 365 Copilot to an astounding million users across their Global 2000 clients and various industries.

Beyond the immediate benefits, this partnership holds immense potential for India's burgeoning AI landscape. With AI projected to contribute a colossal *$450-500 billion* to the country's GDP by 2025, Cognizant is investing heavily to harness the power of this transformative technology.

## Cognizant's CEO on Generative AI
Cognizant's CEO, Ravi Kumar S, envisions Generative AI as a "game-changer" that unlocks unprecedented innovation, efficiency, and growth opportunities. The company's commitment to GenAI, backed by a billion-dollar investment over the next three years, underscores their unwavering belief in its transformative potential.

Through Cognizant's Synapse skilling program, 35,000 developers have already mastered GitHub Copilot, with another 40,000 set to embrace its capabilities. This training initiative lays the foundation for widespread AI adoption and innovation in India.

## Conclusion

As the world charges towards AI's future, Cognizant and Microsoft stand as pioneers, guiding businesses through this digital transformation journey. Their partnership promises to unleash the boundless potential of AI, paving the way for a future where efficiency, innovation, and growth soar to unprecedented heights.
