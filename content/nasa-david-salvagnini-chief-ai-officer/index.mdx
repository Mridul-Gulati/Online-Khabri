In a game-changing move, NASA has appointed David Salvagnini as its first-ever Chief Artificial Intelligence (AI) Officer, expanding his current role as Chief Data Officer. This strategic appointment marks a bold step in NASA's relentless pursuit of innovation and efficiency through the integration of AI technology in its missions.

## From Intelligence to the Cosmos

Salvagnini, a seasoned technology visionary, brings decades of experience to his new post. His distinguished career spans the intelligence community, including service in the Office of the Director of National Intelligence, and a 21-year tenure in the Air Force as a communications and computer systems officer.

> "NASA has a long and successful history of using AI safely," remarked NASA Administrator Bill Nelson. "With David's guidance, we aim to harness the transformative power of AI to accelerate our pace of discovery and enhance our operations."

## AI in the Cosmos and Beyond

Salvagnini's mission is to spearhead NASA's AI strategy, aligning with President Biden's Executive Order on the responsible development and use of AI. He will lead the agency's efforts to ensure AI is ethically employed, fostering innovation, and developing tools and training to advance AI capabilities.

Beyond its celestial pursuits, NASA's AI initiatives will also impact Earth-bound endeavors. From analyzing Earth science imagery to facilitating communication with spacecraft such as the Perseverance Mars rover, AI plays a pivotal role in NASA's terrestrial and extraterrestrial operations.

## Collaborating for Cosmic Advancements

> "AI has been a game-changer for NASA," noted Salvagnini. 
"By collaborating with experts across government, academia, and industry, we can remain at the cutting edge of AI technology, pushing the boundaries of human ingenuity."

## A Legacy of AI Leadership

Before Salvagnini's appointment, NASA's Chief Scientist Kate Calvin served as the acting responsible AI official, highlighting the agency's unwavering commitment to the responsible use of AI in its scientific and exploratory missions.

Salvagnini's appointment is a testament to NASA's unwavering belief in the transformative potential of AI. As he steps into this groundbreaking role, he will guide the agency towards a future where AI empowers its pursuit of knowledge and exploration, benefiting humanity for generations to come.
