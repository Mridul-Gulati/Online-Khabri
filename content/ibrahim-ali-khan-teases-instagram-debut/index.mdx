Ibrahim Ali Khan, the son of Saif Ali Khan and Amrita Singh, has captured the limelight even before his Bollywood debut. on monday, he was spotted in the city, drawing the attention of the paparazzi. As they pursued him into a building, they exchanged greetings. Ibrahim gracefully posed for them before dashing away. Amidst the frenzy, he was asked if he had an Instagram account.

## Instagram Curiosity

Unveiling a surprising twist, Ibrahim responded that he had none. However, he then posed an intriguing question: 
> "Should I join Instagram?" The photographers' enthusiastic affirmation prompted him to announce, "Tomorrow at 11 am."

## Private Instagram Existence

Intriguingly, even before his public announcement, Ibrahim maintained a private Instagram account, (iak). Notable Bollywood stars, including Aryan Khan, Mrunal Thakur, and Alia Bhatt, are among his followers. His sister, Sara Ali Khan, frequently tags him on her active Instagram handle.

## Fan Anticipation

Ibrahim's Instagram tease has ignited excitement among his fans, who are eagerly awaiting potential updates on his upcoming projects. Comments such as
> "Yes!! He's joining Instagram" and "Very handsome" reflect their anticipation.

## Upcoming Projects

On the cinematic front, Ibrahim is poised to take Bollywood by storm with 'Sarzameen,' co-starring Kajol and Prithviraj Sukumaran. Directed by Boman Irani's son, Kayoze Irani, the film promete a captivating cinematic experience. Ibrahim is also reportedly working on another project with Khushi Kapoor.

## Heartfelt Tribute to Amrita Singh

In a related development, Sara Ali Khan recently composed a heartfelt poem for her mother, Amrita Singh, on her 66th birthday.
