## Preparation for "The Broken News 2"

As Sanjeeta Bhattacharya gracefully navigated the transition from hacker to news anchor in "The Broken News 2," she meticulously crafted her character. Balancing the filming of "Jawan," she immersed herself in studying news reporters, observing their nuances and mastering the art of delivering news with a poker face while conveying depth of emotion.

## Experiences on Set

Her time on set was marked by camaraderie and inspiration. Sonali Bendre's strength and humility left a lasting impression, while Jaideep Ahlawat's raw talent and approachable nature fostered a bond of respect. Shreya Pilgaonkar, a kindred spirit, offered invaluable friendship and support.

## Navigating False News

In the age of misinformation, Sanjeeta stresses the importance of critical thinking and seeking reputable sources. She advocates for responsible news consumption, emphasizing the potential consequences of sharing unverified information.

## The Impact of "Jawan"

The unprecedented success of "Jawan" has been a transformative force for Sanjeeta's career. It has not only elevated her visibility but also opened doors to more substantial and challenging roles. Beyond fame, she cherishes the profound friendships she has forged within the cast and crew.

Recalling her unforgettable experience with Shah Rukh Khan, Sanjeeta describes his thoughtful gesture of bringing a guitar and microphone to set so she could perform for the team. His heartfelt compliment, 
> "The goddess is in your voice," 

ignited within her the inspiration to contribute her voice to the film's soundtrack.

## Measuring Success

Sanjeeta measures success not by external accolades but by the freedom to pursue her passions and make meaningful choices in her work. She strives to be a beacon of vulnerability and an embodiment of perseverance.

## Dream Collaborations

Her wish list of collaborators includes luminaries such as Sanjay Leela Bhansali, Karan Johar, and Anurag Kashyap. She eagerly anticipates the opportunity to delve into different cinematic worlds, explore regional cinema, and even transcend borders to collaborate with international talents like Timothee Chalamet.

## Upcoming Projects

Sanjeeta's creative journey is brimming with exciting projects. She stars as the protagonist in an upcoming Bengali film, showcases her versatility in Amazon Prime's "Django," and gears up for the release of "Adbhut." Musically, she is working on her enchanting new single, "Ratiya," a fusion of Afrobeats, Indian classical, and Hindi pop. Collaborations with independent artists are also on the horizon, promising a vibrant and diverse musical landscape for the passionate singer-turned-actress.
