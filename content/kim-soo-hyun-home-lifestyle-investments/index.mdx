In the heart of Seoul's vibrant Seongsu-dong district lies Kim Soo Hyun's opulent primary residence at Galleria Forêt. This grand masterpiece of contemporary architecture spans a breathtaking 217 square meters, adorned with floor-to-ceiling windows that bathe the space in ethereal light. Acquired for a staggering 4.02 billion KRW in 2013, this prized possession has witnessed a phenomenal surge in value, now estimated at an astounding 13.5 billion KRW, a testament to the actor's astute investment strategy.

## A Thriving Investment Portfolio

Beyond his Seongsu-dong estate, Kim Soo Hyun's real estate portfolio extends to a second residence at Seoul Forest Trimage, acquired in 2014 for 3.02 billion KRW. This exquisite sanctuary offers a serene haven from the bustling city, boasting a spacious 170 square meters of living space. Earlier this year, the actor further expanded his property empire with the acquisition of a lavish private penthouse, valued at 8.8 billion KRW.
