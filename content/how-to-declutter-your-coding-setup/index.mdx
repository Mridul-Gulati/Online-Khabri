## 6. Effective Cable Management Tips üîå

Messy cables can make even the most organized desk feel chaotic. Here‚Äôs how to keep them under control:

1. **Cable Ties**: Use Velcro or plastic ties to bundle cables together neatly. These are reusable, adjustable, and can be moved as needed.
2. **Cable Clips**: Attach clips to your desk or wall to keep cables in place and prevent tangling.
3. **Cable Sleeves**: Bundle multiple cables into a single, neat tube to protect them from damage and make them easier to manage.
4. **Cable Management Boxes**: Hide power strips and excess cables in a sleek management box.
5. **Cable Trays**: Install under-desk trays to keep cables off the floor and out of sight.
6. **Cable Labels**: Label your cables to quickly identify them, especially helpful if you have multiple devices connected.
