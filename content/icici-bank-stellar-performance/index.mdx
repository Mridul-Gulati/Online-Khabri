Mumbai's banking landscape witnessed a surge in momentum as ICICI Bank unveiled its exceptional financial performance for the quarter ending March 2024.

## Surge in Consolidated Net Profit

[ICICI Bank](https://www.icicibank.com/) soared to new heights, achieving an impressive 18.5% leap in its consolidated net profit. The bank's bottom line reached a commendable Rs ** 11,672 crore**, eclipsing the Rs 9,853 crore recorded during the corresponding period in 2023.

## Robust Standalone Profit Growth

The bank's standalone operations also showcased remarkable resilience. Net profit after tax surged by 17.4%, reaching Rs 10,708 crore, up from Rs 9,122 crore in the previous year. This substantial growth underscores the bank's unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional value to its stakeholders.

## Driving Force: Loan Growth and NIM

ICICI Bank's core net interest income experienced a commendable 8.1% increase to **Rs 19,093 crore**. This surge was fueled by a robust 16.8% growth in loans. Despite a modest compression in net interest margin to 4.40%, the bank's lending prowess remained evident.

## Non-Interest Income Strengthens

Excluding treasury performance, ICICI Bank's non-interest income showcased a **15.7% surge, reaching Rs 5,930 crore**. This growth is a testament to the bank's diverse revenue streams and its ability to generate non-lending income.

## Provisions Halved: A Sign of Confidence

ICICI Bank's provisions dwindled by more than half to Rs 718 crore, indicating the bank's confidence in its asset quality and resilience against potential risks. This conservative approach demonstrates its commitment to long-term stability and profitability.
